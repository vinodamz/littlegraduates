Hey there, eco-champions! Here’s how our kiddos in the best playschool Little Graduates Kaloor turned a regular school day into an Earth-loving fiesta:




---




#### 🌱 Plant-a-Friend Safari 🙂



* Each child tucked a baby sapling into the soil and whispered a name to it (“Captain Leafy,” “Princess Mango”… ☺️).
* We strolled around campus hugging trunks, sniffing leaves, and guessing who’s who in the tree neighborhood.  
  *Why?* When a tree has a name, watering it feels like visiting a friend. 💧💕



![](https://thelittlegraduates.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/20250605_113415-577x1024.jpg)




---




#### 🏖️ Sandpit Spring-Clean 🧹✨



* Armed with teeny rakes, our crew cleared fallen leaves from the sandpit—discovering millipedes, seed pods, and the odd forgotten toy.  
  *Hidden win:* Stewardship + surprise biology lesson = happy, muddy smiles! 😄



![](https://thelittlegraduates.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/20250605_112327-768x1024.jpg)




---




#### 🎈 “Pop the Planet” Paint Blast 🎨



* Balloons filled with blue & green paint, a blank canvas, and three… two… one—POP! 💥
* Splats turned into gorgeous mini Earths while we chatted about oceans (blue 🌊) and continents (green 🌿).  
  *Take-home wow:* Even playful messes can make planet-worthy art!


![](https://thelittlegraduates.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/20250605_130007-577x1024.jpg)




---




### 🌟 Ripples That Last



* Saplings grow alongside our little graduates playschool (best classroom decor ever!).
* Sandpit clean-ups are now a weekly ritual.
* Those splatter-paint Earth canvases brighten our lobby, whispering “Care for me!” every day. 🌎❤️



![](https://thelittlegraduates.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/20250605_171635-1-1024x768.jpg)
