**June 4, 2025** — a date that will always hold a special place in the hearts of many parents, children, and teachers at **Little Graduates Early Learning Centre in Kaloor, Kochi**. Today marked the official start of the new academic year, and with it, dozens of tiny stories began — each filled with excitement, a few butterflies, and plenty of heartwarming moments.



## 🌼 A Big Day for Little Ones



For many children, this was their first ever experience stepping into a school environment. Leaving the comfort of home and familiar faces, they took their first independent steps into a whole new world — one filled with colors, toys, books, songs, and friendly teachers.



Some children walked in confidently, eager to explore. Others held on tightly to mom or dad, not quite ready to let go. A few tears were shed (which is absolutely natural), but very soon replaced by smiles, giggles, and curious eyes as they slowly settled into their new space.



At **Little Graduates Preschool & Playschool in Kaloor**, we deeply understand these emotions — for both children and parents. The first day at school is not just a new beginning for the child, but also for the family. We make sure this transition is smooth, safe, and filled with positivity.



## 🌱 How We Prepare for the First Day



The first day isn’t just about showing up and starting school. It’s about **settling gently**. Here’s how we help children adjust:



* Warm welcome at the school gate with smiling staff
* Personalized classroom tours to explore new surroundings
* Calm, child-led settling-in activities like puzzles, sensory play, and music
* Flexible schedules for the first few days to avoid overwhelming the child
* Open communication with parents, with constant updates & feedback



The goal is simple: help each child feel safe, loved, and confident.



## 🎯 The Power of a Strong Start



A positive first-day experience helps build:



* Emotional security
* Trust in teachers & caregivers
* Early independence
* A natural love for learning



At **Little Graduates**, we follow the principles of Montessori, blended with the **Kreedo Curriculum**, which allows every child to explore at their own pace. This helps even the shyest child gradually open up, make friends, and begin enjoying the school journey.

## ❤️ A Word to Parents



We know it isn’t easy handing your little one over on the first day. You’ve raised them with love and care, and trusting someone else feels emotional. But this is also a beautiful step forward.



Here are a few tips for parents during this phase:



* Stay calm & positive — your child senses your energy
* Keep goodbyes short, sweet, and confident
* Allow time for your child to adjust — every child is different
* Communicate openly with teachers if you have concerns



Remember, settling in is a process. Some children adjust in a day, some in a week. Both are normal. The important thing is — we’re here for both you and your child every step of the way.
