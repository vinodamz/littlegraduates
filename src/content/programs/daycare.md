Our Daycare program offers a home-like environment where children feel safe, loved, and free to be themselves. Whether your child stays for a few extra hours or spends the whole day, our space is designed to provide a smooth, nurturing rhythm between care and learning.
Children follow a predictable routine with time for rest, meals, play, and enrichment activities. The space is cozy and inviting, staffed by caregivers who genuinely enjoy being with children and understand their emotional needs. The goal? A calm, structured, and loving environment that supports the whole child — emotionally, socially, and physically.
Mixed-age interactions promote cooperation and empathy, while flexible routines allow for individual attention. Families can choose from daily or weekly packages based on their schedule and needs.
A Daycare child’s daily rhythm includes:

* Nap or quiet rest time
* Nutritious meals and snack breaks
* Indoor and outdoor free play
* Music, stories, and games
* Hands-on exploration with age-appropriate materials
* Personal care and hygiene routines
* Circle time for bonding and reflection
* Seasonal and festival celebrations
