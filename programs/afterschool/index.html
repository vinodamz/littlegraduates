Afterschool Program

Learning doesn’t stop when the school day ends — it simply shifts gears. Our Afterschool Program is a space where children wind down, explore interests, and continue growing in a relaxed, supportive environment. It’s designed for working parents who want a safe, enriching space for their children in the late afternoons. From creative expression to life skills and quiet reading corners, the program is flexible yet purposeful. Children can engage in guided activities or choose their own rhythm, allowing them to feel both free and grounded. The mixed-age group setting helps build social connections and encourages peer learning across ages, with a balance of structured engagement and open-ended play. An Afterschool child’s experience includes:

  • Art and craft activities
  • Storytelling and puppet time
  • Cooking, gardening, and practical life tasks
  • Music, movement, and yoga sessions
  • Quiet zones for puzzles, books, and reflection
  • Homework support (for Class 1 & 2)
  • Outdoor play and exploration
  • Festival- and theme-based monthly workshops
