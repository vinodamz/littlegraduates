Curriculum · 2026–27

What your child learns

A clear snapshot of our Montessori-based 6T curriculum, built with Kreedo and fully aligned with NEP 2020 & the NCF. Children learn concrete-to-abstract, at their own pace — here's how it grows from Playgroup to UKG.

Level 0

Playgroup

1.5–3 yrs View programme →

Pre-writing & art

Colouring, tracing, drawing, and plenty of hands-on art & craft.

Vocabulary

Naming the world around them — objects, food, fruits & vegetables, animals & birds, vehicles, plants, professions, festivals, instruments and seasons.

Communication

Singing rhymes, listening to stories, talking about pictures, recognising letter sounds, and numbers 0–10.

Level 1

Nursery

3–4 yrs View programme →

English & phonics

Picture talk, rhymes & stories, tracing curves & lines, and phonics sound sets 1–5 (c-a-p-t-i-n … y-j-x-q), first & ending sounds, and uppercase letters.

Maths

More & less, number symbols 0–20, counting 1–20, and ordering & sequencing numbers.

Sensorial

Identifying objects, sizes, colours, shapes and simple patterns.

The world around us

Body, clothes & home, food, animals & birds, plants & trees, vehicles, professions, seasons, festivals and traffic signs.

Level 2

LKG

4–5 yrs View programme →

English

Letters & sounds a–z, 3-letter words & sentences, blend & sight words, consonant phonograms, homophones, and long & short vowels.

Maths

Numbers 1–100, place value (units → thousands), sequencing, counting backwards, addition with carryover, skip counting and 3-digit numbers.

Sensorial

Dimensions, colour shades, 2D & 3D shapes, patterns and directions.

The world around us

Body & feelings, living / non-living, plants & animals & lifecycles, professions, transport, seasons, months & days, landforms, and culture & festivals.

Level 3

UKG

5–6 yrs View programme →

English

Rhymes & stories, all sounds a–z, CVC / blend / sight words & sentences, long-vowel & phonogram words, an introduction to nouns & verbs, and writing simple answers.

Maths

Numbers 1–1000, place value, comparing & ordering, addition & subtraction (with carry & borrow), skip counting, telling time, and single-digit multiplication.

Sensorial & reasoning

2D/3D shapes & their properties, patterns, odd-one-out, mirror images, what-comes-next and dimensions.

The world around us

My body, family & feelings, food groups, plants & animals, transport, cultural India, the Earth & landforms, and planets, continents & oceans.
