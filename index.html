Parents ask

Frequently asked questions

What ages does Little Graduates accept?

We welcome children from 1.5 to 6 years across Playgroup, Nursery, LKG and UKG, plus daycare and afterschool options.

Where is Little Graduates located?

Kattayil Road, Near Kaloor, Elamakara, Kochi 682017 — in the heart of the city.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a 1:12 ratio so every child is seen, heard, and gently guided.

What curriculum do you follow?

A Montessori-based 6T curriculum built with Kreedo, fully aligned with NEP 2020 and the NCF.

Are admissions open for 2026?

Yes — 2026 admissions are open. Book a free tour or WhatsApp us to begin.

Do you have outdoor play areas?

Yes. Sky, mud, grass and room to roam — a rare gem in Kochi.
