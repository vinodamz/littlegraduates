Programs

From first steps to first day of school

1.5 to 3 years

Playgroup

Children thrive in a mixed-age Montessori environment where learning happens naturally through observation, hands-on exploration, and social collaboration. The program nurtures independence, empathy, and foundational learning through purposeful play.

3 to 4 years

Nursery

Students explore early literacy, numeracy, and environmental awareness through active, hands-on learning. In a mixed-age setup, they thrive emotionally and intellectually while building independence and empathy.

4 to 5 years

Lower Kindergarten - LKG

Students begin structured learning in literacy, numeracy, and environment-based themes while developing independence, confidence, and leadership in a collaborative, mixed-age environment.

5 to 6 years

Upper Kindergarten (UKG, Ages 5 – 6 years)

Students build strong academic readiness for primary school while enhancing self-regulation, collaboration, and leadership — all rooted in active, purposeful learning.

Curriculum & the 6T framework

Aligned with NEP. Built for joy.

Our curriculum, built in partnership with Kreedo, is fully aligned with India's New Education Policy (NEP) and the National Curriculum Framework (NCF). The 6T framework: Toys & Games · Theory · Timetable · Training · Technology · Teamwork.

Frequently asked questions

What ages does Little Graduates accept?

We welcome children from 1.5 to 6 years across Playgroup, Nursery, LKG and UKG, plus daycare and afterschool options.

Where is Little Graduates located?

Kattayil Road, Near Kaloor, Elamakara, Kochi 682017 — in the heart of the city.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a 1:12 ratio so every child is seen, heard, and gently guided.

What curriculum do you follow?

A Montessori-based 6T curriculum built with Kreedo, fully aligned with NEP 2020 and the NCF.
