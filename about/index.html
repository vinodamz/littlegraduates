About us

A warm start. A joyful future.

A warm, joyful preschool in the heart of Kaloor, Kochi — for children aged 1.5 to 6.

A warm, joyful preschool in the heart of Kaloor, Kochi — for children aged 1.5 to 6. We believe the early years shape everything that comes next. Our 6T Curriculum blends Montessori principles, play-based learning, and hands-on skill building — so every child grows in confidence, curiosity, and kindness.

With experienced teachers, modern facilities, and a strong focus on emotional and social development, your child won't just learn here. They'll feel at home.

Beyond Montessori — children learn by doing

We go beyond traditional Montessori with daily hands-on sessions, STEM, art, storytelling, and real-world exploration. With authentic, child-sized Montessori materials, a NEP 2020 & NCF-aligned approach, and child-led play, every day builds a confident, lifelong learner.

How we teach

Image of the child

Teachers build on what children already know — by closely observing their interests, having meaningful one-on-one conversations, and gently inviting them to explore deeper.

Positive discipline

Discipline comes from guidance, not punishment. We don't allow corporal punishment of any kind. Instead, we help children understand boundaries with empathy, respect, and gentle redirection.

Environment matters

A thoughtfully prepared space — calm, beautiful, accessible — encourages independence, focus, and exploration. When the space is respectful and nurturing, children thrive.
