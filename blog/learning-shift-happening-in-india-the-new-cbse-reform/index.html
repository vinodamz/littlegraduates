Learning Shift happening in India?- the new CBSE Reform 🌱

14 October 2025

🌱 Building Children for Life, Not Just for Exams

There’s a big shift happening in education.

CBSE is moving away from rote learning — the old “memorize and repeat” model — toward competency-based learning.

💡 80% of exams will now test how well children can think, understand, and apply, not just remember.

It’s a huge change for schools and teachers.
But here’s the thing — this is exactly what we’ve already been doing at Little Graduates.

⸻

🧩 CBSE’s new direction
• Focus on understanding, not memorizing
• Real-life learning and application
• Teacher training for conceptual teaching
• Internal assessments and practical evaluation

⸻

✨ How Little Graduates is already aligned
• We encourage exploration and curiosity instead of rote answers.
• Children learn through real experiences — pouring, planting, cooking, sorting, and observing.
• Our teachers focus on connection, patience, and understanding each child’s pace.
• We build life skills — confidence, empathy, independence — along with academics.

⸻

📘 What this means for parents:
Your child’s early learning at Little Graduates is already in sync with the education reforms India is embracing.
They’re growing up ready for life, not just for exams.

When learning starts with curiosity and joy, everything else naturally follows. 🌼
