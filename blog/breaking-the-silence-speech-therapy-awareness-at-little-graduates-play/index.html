Breaking the Silence: Speech Therapy Awareness at Little Graduates Playschool, Kochi

11 August 2025

At Little Graduates Early Learning Centre, the Montessori in Kochi, we believe learning isn’t just about ABCs and numbers—it’s also about helping every child find their voice. On 9th August 2025, we hosted a Speech Therapy Awareness Session for parents and caregivers, led by an experienced speech-language pathologist Mrs Gladin, to shed light on the importance of early detection and intervention for speech delays.

Why We Organized This Session

Speech and language skills are the foundation of learning, communication, and social confidence. Yet, many parents aren’t sure when a speech delay is “normal” and when it needs professional attention. We wanted to bridge that gap by:

  • Explaining early signs of speech delay in toddlers and preschoolers.
  • Sharing simple, everyday activities parents can do at home to encourage speech.
  • Highlighting how speech therapy works and why starting early matters.

Key Takeaways for Parents

During the session, our guest expert covered:

  • Milestones: What to expect from 1–5 years in terms of speech and language.
  • Red Flags: When to consider professional evaluation.
  • Practical Tips: Storytelling, songs, and interactive play as tools for speech growth.
  • Myth Busting: Clearing misconceptions like “boys talk late” or “it will happen naturally.”

Parents left with actionable knowledge and confidence to support their child’s communication journey.

Our Commitment to Inclusive Learning

At Little Graduates, we welcome children with diverse learning needs, including those with speech delays. Our teachers are trained to use Montessori-inspired, play-based learning methods that integrate language development naturally into everyday activities.

By collaborating with professionals in speech therapy for children in Kochi, we ensure that families have access to the right resources, guidance, and encouragement.

Final Thoughts

A child’s voice is their world. The earlier we help them express it, the better their future communication, learning, and relationships will be. We are proud to be a school that not only teaches but also cares deeply about each child’s developmental milestones.

If you’re looking for a playschool in Kochi that supports speech development and provides resources for early intervention, visit us at Little Graduates Early Learning Centre.
